Delete Your User Data

Steps to Request Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your user account and all associated data from KilySpeak AI, please contact our support team using the following instructions:

  1. Send an email to contact-us@nerayouth.com.
  2. Use the subject line: "Request to Delete User Data".
  3. Include the following details in your email:
    • Your full name
    • Your email address associated with the KilySpeak AI account
    • Your reason for requesting data deletion (optional)

Once we receive your request, our support team will process it and notify you when your account and data have been deleted.

Sample Email Template

You can use the template below to send your request:

To: contact-us@nerayouth.com

Subject: Request to Delete User Data

Dear KilySpeak AI Support Team,

I am writing to request the deletion of my user account and all associated data from KilySpeak AI. Below are my account details:

  • Full Name: [Your Full Name]
  • Email Address: [Your Email Address]

If you require any additional information, please let me know. I understand that this process is irreversible and that all my data will be permanently deleted.

Thank you for your assistance.

Best regards,

[Your Full Name]
